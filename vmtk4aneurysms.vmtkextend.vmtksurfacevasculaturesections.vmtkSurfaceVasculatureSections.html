def _get_inlet_and_outlets(self):

Compute inlet and outlets centers of vascular surface.

Based on the surface and assuming that the inlet is the open profile with largest area, return a tuple with two lists: the first includes the coordinates of the inlet and the second the coordinates of the outlets.
